Trekking in Nepal

Curated and Responsible Adventure

Curated by a team of Trekking in Nepal Experts, Mountain Monarch is one of the best adventure companies for Nepal trekking trips. Our competent and professional team has been leading countless successful adventure trekking in Himalaya for over 2 decades. It has helped us grow to become a genuinely reliable adventure company to run Nepal treks to peak climbing expeditions in the Himalayas. Distinct responsible travel practices with proper safety backup are our main signature priorities in organizing any adventure trekking and climbing package tours.

Responsible Trekking in Nepal with Proper Safety

Nepal exhibits incomparable natural beauties decorated by the long preserved local cultures. Mountain Monarch, being a reputed trekking agency in Nepal, has not only introduced these gifts to the adventurers but has held a reputation for unfolding natural and cultural aesthetics like never before. We always make sure Oxygen Cylinders and Comprehensive Medical Kit are accompanied by experienced and medically trained guides on each trip for the safe adventure holidays of a lifetime. These are extremely necessary, especially during high-altitude treks to climbing trips in the Himalayas. More than that, we have committed to conducting socially responsible best adventure tours in Nepal. Our "1 Trekker:1 Porter " on Trekking in Himalaya to climbing expedition is a gesture of responsible travel that has been encouraged and praised by many of our travelers. This responsible travel policy has helped to lessen the burden of the porters and also create employment opportunities for many others, a way for us to give back to our local community. Also, concerning environmental efforts, we support sustainable tourism that helps to promote and preserve the Himalayas. The effective practices of these safety measures and responsible travel guidelines on organizing Nepal adventure trekking holidays, have gained us a glowing reputation in part of Trip Advisor and Lonely Planet's continuous recommendations.
